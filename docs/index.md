# CTF Writeups

This is mostly a place for me to keep notes on CTFs as I prepare for OSCP. My objective is to practice my notetaking and reporting skills. If my notes are not clear or something isn't working, there is always a link to ippsec's YouTube channel as well as 0xdf's blog at the top of every HTB write up.

I'm also creating a cheatsheet section for a few different tools / use cases. These are tools that I use often enough that I don't want to google / LLM every time, but not often enough that I have memorized the syntax.

The Daily Brief section is mostly to keep me disciplined about researching current events rather than just doing old CTFs.
